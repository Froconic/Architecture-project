Our Process

Work with clients on projects that range in scope from: Color, background, fixture & furniture finishes and textile consulting to full-scale remodels and ground up architectural interiors design & build, ground up architectural home design & build, with a focus on high-end residential projects, in addition to consulting on design for condo/multi-family unit development, hotel concessions and other commercial spaces. Our professional interior and architectural designers will take into account all your wishes and create the interior or home design of your dream! Increasingly, the customer comes to the decision to transfer the management and organization of the design and construction process into one. This is due to the need for continuous coordination of actions of all participants in the construction and design process. Namely, — General contractor; — Contractors and subcontractors; — Material suppliers; — General Designer, - Engineers and other designers. In order for best result and success for your construction and design project, you will need professionals who know how the business goes — during every phase. Heartwood Build & Design and it's experienced professional construction and design consultants provide effective leadership, close attention to detail and a mutually beneficial partnership that you can rely on during all phases of your project implementation.
